Frequently Asked Questions

How long does customization take?+
Most personalized pieces are engraved or printed within 48 hours of order confirmation, then shipped with luxury wrap included.
Can I preview my design before paying?+
Yes — every product opens in our Live Customization Studio, where you can upload a photo, add text, and see a real-time preview before adding to cart.
What if I make a spelling mistake?+
You can edit your design directly in the studio preview any time before checkout. Once an order is placed for engraving, changes may incur a small remake fee.
Do you offer bulk customization for corporate orders?+
Yes — our Corporate Gifting flow supports logo uploads, bulk name lists via spreadsheet, and a dedicated account manager for orders above 20 units.
